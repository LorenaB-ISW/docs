# Product Overview

## What is Simba Intelligence?

Simba Intelligence is an **AI-powered data platform** that transforms how organizations access and analyze their data. Instead of requiring technical expertise to write complex SQL queries or navigate multiple data systems, Simba Intelligence allows users to ask questions in plain English and get instant, intelligent answers from their data sources.

Think of Simba Intelligence as having a knowledgeable data analyst on your team who can:

- Instantly understand what data you need based on natural language descriptions
- Automatically connect to and configure your databases
- Query multiple data sources simultaneously
- Provide answers in the format that makes the most sense for your question
